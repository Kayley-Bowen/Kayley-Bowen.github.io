The Bedford Mission Garden: Home


The Bedford Mission Garden is a 1600 square foot community garden located in Bedford, NH. The garden is completely volunteer based and funded by donations and fundraisers. The produce grown is donated directly to the NH Food Bank. The NH Food Bank then distributes the food to any of the over 400 locations they provide food to, ultimately reaching hundreds of food-insecure individuals across the state.

Bedford Food Pantry

Feeding America

Feeding America is a nationwide nonprofit which supports hunger-relief efforts, such as the Bedford Mission Garden, the NH Food Bank, New Horizons of Manchester, and more.

NH Food Bank

We are working with the NH Food Bank to distribute produce we grow to over 400 locations in the state of New Hampshire.
